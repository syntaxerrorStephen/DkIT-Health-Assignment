About Our Facilities

Healthy Campus

Welcome to Sport @ DkIT - an empowering fitness initiative proudly situated in the heart of Louth! Committed to enhancing the overall well-being of our students, Sport @ DkIT is not just a gym; it's a dynamic community fostering a culture of health, vitality, and camaraderie.


Our state-of-the-art gym is not only well-equipped but also freely accessible, reflecting our dedication to providing every student with the opportunity to embark on their fitness journey without financial constraints. We believe that a healthy body is the foundation for a successful academic and personal life.


More than just a place to break a sweat, Sport @ DkIT is a hub of positive energy and inclusivity. We understand the demands of student life and aim to create an environment where physical activity is not just a routine but an integral part of the DkIT experience.


Join us in embracing a holistic approach to health and fitness. Whether you're a fitness enthusiast or a beginner taking the first steps on your wellness journey, Sport @ DkIT is here to support and inspire you. Let's build a healthier, happier, and more active campus together!
